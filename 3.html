Etat Libre d'Orange, Experimentum Crusis

Experimentum Crucis by Etat Libre d'Orange is a Woody Aromatic fragrance for women and men. This is a new fragrance. Experimentum Crucis was launched in 2019. The nose behind this fragrance is Quentin Bisch. Top notes are Cumin, Apple and Litchi; middle notes are Rose, Jasmine and Honey; base notes are Akigalawood, Patchouli and Musk.
